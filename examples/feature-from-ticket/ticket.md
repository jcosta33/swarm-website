# Intake: Contact form on the marketing site

Pasted verbatim from ACME-482. The spec interprets; this file preserves what
was actually asked.

> **ACME-482 — Contact form on the marketing site**
> Reporter: Dana M. (Support) · Priority: High
>
> Customers keep emailing the old support@ alias, which nobody monitors since
> the helpdesk move. We need a contact form on `/contact`:
>
> - name, email, message
> - validate the email address
> - messages should land somewhere the support team actually sees them
> - attachments would help for bug reports (screenshots)
> - probably needs a captcha so we don't drown in bot spam
> - nice-to-have: send the customer a copy of their message
